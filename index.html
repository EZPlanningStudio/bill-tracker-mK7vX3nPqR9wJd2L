👋 Welcome to Bill Tracker!

This app helps you organize your bills and subscriptions so you always know what's due and when. Everything is saved locally on your device — simple, fast, no account needed.

🌐 Recommended Browser

This app was built and tested in Google Chrome. We recommend using Chrome or Safari for the full experience, including the automatic backup feature.

Other browsers may work, but some features (such as automatic backup file saving) might not be available.

✨ Help Icons

Throughout the app you'll find small clickable icons next to titles, filters, and form fields. Click any of them to get contextual help for that area — without losing your place. Some examples: ✨ 🎛️ 📋 💡 💾

💾 Backup & Offline Use

📱
Works offline
No internet needed. All your data is saved locally on your device.
🔄
No automatic sync
Because data is local, it doesn't sync automatically between devices.
📦
Moving to another device
Export your backup file from the Backup page and import it on the new device.
⚙️
Auto-backup in Chrome
Set your auto-save location in the Backup page so your data is always protected.
